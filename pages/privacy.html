Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026. This is a plain-language summary; adapt it with your own legal counsel before going live commercially.

The short version

AbroadReady is designed to work without accounts. The scholarship board, quiz, SOP builder and assistant all run in your browser. We don't require you to sign up to use them, and we don't sell personal data.

What stays on your device

  • Quiz answers are used only to filter results and are not sent to a server.
  • SOP drafts are saved in your browser's local storage so you don't lose your work. They stay on your device and you can clear them any time.
  • Assistant questions are answered from local data in your browser.

What we may collect (if you enable it)

  • Newsletter: if you subscribe, we store the email you provide to send updates. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Contact forms: if you message us, we keep what you send in order to reply.
  • Basic analytics: if the operator enables analytics, aggregate, non-identifying usage stats may be collected.

Third-party links

Every "Apply" button sends you to the official scholarship provider's website. Those sites have their own privacy policies, which govern anything you do there.

Your choices

You can clear locally stored data by clearing your browser storage for this site. You can request deletion of any email or message you sent us by contacting our team.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us via the contact page.
