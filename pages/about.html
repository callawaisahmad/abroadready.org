Our Mission

Making scholarships findable

We believe money should never be the reason a talented student doesn't study abroad.

AbroadReady is a free scholarship board that gathers international scholarships into one place — with clear eligibility, required documents, benefits, deadlines and direct apply links — so students spend less time hunting and more time applying.

Why we built it

Scholarship information is scattered across hundreds of government portals, university pages and PDFs. Deadlines are buried, eligibility is confusing, and the best opportunities are often missed simply because nobody found them in time. We pull the essential details into a single, filterable board and keep the status of each one current.

What we stand for

🆓

Free, always

The student side of AbroadReady is free and always will be.

🎯

Accurate

Details are compiled from official sources and links are checked regularly.

🔒

Private

No selling of your data. What you type stays in your browser.

How it works

Take the two-minute quiz or head straight to the board, filter by programme, intake, region, field and funding, then open any scholarship for the full picture and the official apply link. Our SOP Builder and assistant help you prepare a stronger application once you've found your match.
